How to Run "Student Club & Event Management System"

1. Start XAMPP
   - Open the XAMPP Control Panel.
   - Click "Start" next to **Apache** and **MySQL**.
   - Ensure both turn green.

2. Setup Database
   - Open your browser and go to: http://localhost/phpmyadmin
   - Click **"New"** in the left sidebar.
   - Enter Database Name: `university_club_db`
   - Click **"Create"**.
   - Click the **"Import"** tab at the top.
   - Click **"Choose File"** and select the file: 
     `C:\xampp\htdocs\student_club\database.sql`
   - Click **"Import"** at the bottom of the page.

3. Run the Website
   - Open your web browser.
   - Go to: http://localhost/student_club/
